What is MACD?

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) is a technical analysis indicator developed by Gerald Appel. It is a trend following momentum indicator. MACD is calculated by subtracting longer moving average from shorter moving average. MACD is often used by traders to detect porential BUY opportunities and exit points. To learn more about MACD read this article on MACD indicator.

What is this filter?

The purpose of this filter is to detect the MACD crossover signals in NSE. This filter scans NIFTY 100 stocks everyday to detect MACD crossover signals. It also tracks the movement of the stock prices after the crossover. If the stock price moves upwards after a bullish crossover, it is termed as a successful call. Similarly if the stock price goes down after a bearish crossover then it is a successful sell signal.
